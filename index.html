ABOUT ALBERT & PUZZLE MANSION

Discover

Albert's cat has been abducted! Enter the mansion and solve puzzles in order to find and rescue the cat! This is a short and lighthearted puzzle game where you move blocks in a certain order to open the door to the next floor. Use your problem solving skills and make your way through the mansion! More puzzle mechanics appear as you traverse the mansion.

Low System

Since the game has a very low system, it can be played easily on almost many computers.

Have Fun

Enjoy the lack of obstructions on your way, except about a trillion territorial cycloptic mages who will repeatedly pepper you with various explosive spells. Socialize with a cacophony of incredibly joyous and friendly characters, none of whom posses crippling depression from living in a world that is falling apart at the seams.
